Identifying signatures of selection in aposematic and cryptic populations of O. granulifera
Used three different selection scans: BayeScan, Ohana, PCAdapt

Scripts for selection scans are provided separedly, as they were performed in different software
and using either R, HPC servers or a combination of both.
